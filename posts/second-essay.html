Reading list

February 2026

So this is my constantly updating reading list. The big ones will have their own categories, while other books will be divided by topic.

2 very important things to note!

1. I will continue to update this as time goes by, so if you happen to see any changes, that's why!

2. I have not read all of these, unfortunately. I am making my way through them, and the ones that I have not read were either : taken from reading suggestions of other online comrades, or directly recommended to me by comrades.

I'll try to put in into categories for now, but be aware that things may change/ move around! Thank you and have a good reading session, remember : "Without revolutionary theory there can be no revolutionary movement."¹- V.I. Lenin
